Background Info

Ila Dhageyso is a project aimed to broadcast and collect feedback from rural areas using interactive social IVR application. Using Tools provided by Microsoft Research in India and in collaboration with Somaliland largest telecommunication company, Telesom and Somaliland Government, was deployed in Novermber 2012 until April 2013. The service was designed to work like social medium where people retrieve information and post responses in interactive fashion. Responses from callers get posted on Youtube and Facebook to connect people with basic phones to popular social sites. The service collected more than 4300 messages from diverse callers. This map interactive map shows small number of audio responses from citizens in SL. We posted location of callers, English translation, and other meta data of the contents. Works best in Firefox & Safari.
